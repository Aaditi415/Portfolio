About Project -

The Online Learning Platform is a mobile app offering accessible, engaging education for all ages. It features a wide range of courses with interactive lessons, AI-powered personalized learning paths, live classes, and community forums. Users can learn offline and instructors can create and manage courses. With 24/7 support, the platform aims to democratize education, providing high-quality learning anytime, anywhere.

About Project -

"Find Your Dream Role Today" is a website for actors and models to find career opportunities. Users create profiles with portfolios and headshots, receiving personalized role recommendations, audition notifications, and casting calls. Features include video audition submissions, networking with industry professionals, and expert tips to enhance skills. The platform streamlines the audition process, making it more efficient and accessible.
