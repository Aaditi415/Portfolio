Bio

Hi, I'm Harshal Bhargude, a creative designer with over 3 years of experience in Graphic Design (GFX), now i do specializing in UI/UX design. My journey started with a passion for visual storytelling, leading me to create compelling visual content and branding. As a graphic designer, I worked on diverse projects, developing a strong understanding of visual hierarchy, color theory, and typography.

Transitioning into UI/UX, I discovered my calling in designing intuitive, user-centered digital experiences. Currently, I focus on bridging aesthetics and functionality, conducting user research, developing wireframes, and creating prototypes that enhance user satisfaction. Proficient in Figma, Adobe XD, Adobe illustrator and Photoshop, I stay updated with the latest design trends and thrive in collaborative environments.
